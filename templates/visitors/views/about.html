Who are we?

Ask a bunch of Nashvillians what their favorite thing is about Nashville, and you'll likely get a different answer from each person. Nashville is an exciting, vibrant, and diverse place; one thing we all agree on is the never-ending supply of fun events and activities that is ever-present here in our city. The abundance of opportunities can be overwhelming at times, and staying in the loop presents a challenge.

Our mission is to help you stay plugged in with all things Nashville, whether it's catching a concert, taking advantage of a local deal, or just finding a good place to grab a drink. Nashboard is your source for news, deals, and events happening locally, and your guide to seizing the day in Nashville.

- The Nashboard Team
